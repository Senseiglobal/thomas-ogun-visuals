Exhibition

Identity & Spirituality

An exhibition about identity, memory and spirituality, with eight physical artworks and two AR experiences.

Concept

The exhibition looks at how identity is shaped by family, culture, belief and personal experience. The physical artworks hold the story, while the AR pieces add moving digital layers.
