Exhibition

Identity & Spirituality

A body of work exploring selfhood, cultural memory and spiritual presence through eight physical artworks and two augmented reality extensions.

Concept

The exhibition frames identity as something layered: inherited, performed, protected and continually reimagined. Physical works act as anchors while AR pieces reveal hidden digital dimensions.
