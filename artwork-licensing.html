Artwork Licensing

Collect a clean high-resolution artwork file for personal display, publishing or commercial use under a clear written licence.

Choose rights that match how the work will be used.

The price reflects the value of the permitted use, not only the image size. Personal collection begins at a lower rate, while commercial and extended licences cover wider audiences, reproduction and business value.

Copyright remains with Thomas Ogun. A digital purchase grants only the permissions written in the selected licence. Copyright transfer or exclusive ownership requires a separate signed agreement.

Every Purchase Includes

A clean file and a clear record.

After payment is verified, the clean artwork and licence certificate are supplied through a private delivery link. The public preview watermark is not present on the purchased file.

Standard delivery is within one business day. Bespoke sizes, CMYK preparation and layered source files require a separate quote.

Protected Rights

Uses that are not permitted

  • Reselling, redistributing or sharing the clean source file
  • Offering the artwork as a stock asset, template or download
  • Minting an NFT without separate written permission
  • Using the artwork for artificial intelligence training or datasets
  • Removing ownership metadata or claiming authorship
  • Using the work for unlawful, defamatory or hateful material

Licence terms are confirmed in writing for each order. Where a proposed use falls outside these options, request a custom licence before publication or production.

Custom or Exclusive Use

Need rights beyond the checkout options?

For exclusivity, copyright assignment, layered files or a use outside the listed terms, contact the studio before purchase.
