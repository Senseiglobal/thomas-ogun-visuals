Artist Overview

Image-making across physical and digital space.

Thomas Ogun is a multidisciplinary visual artist and creative technologist working across illustration, moving image, creative direction and augmented reality.

His practice examines identity, cultural memory and spiritual presence. Traditional drawing and digital image-making provide the visual foundation, while film, sound and interactive technology extend how audiences encounter the work.

The work is connected by a consistent concern: how images can preserve memory, carry emotion and create meaningful encounters across cultures.
