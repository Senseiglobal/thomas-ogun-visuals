About the Artist

I Am a Visual Artist & Creative Technologist

Biography

Thomas Ogun is an interdisciplinary visual artist and creative technologist whose practice explores identity, spirituality, memory, and emerging technologies through painting, cinematography, augmented reality, and creative software.

Thomas Ogun Visuals brings together physical artworks, immersive media, portraiture, product thinking and cultural storytelling to build experiences that feel intimate, rigorous and globally legible.

Mission

To create work that expands how people see themselves, their histories and their possible futures through art and technology.

Vision

To become an internationally recognised studio practice where contemporary art, AR, design and direction meet with clarity and cultural depth.

Creative Philosophy

Presence, Ritual, Image, Interface

Presence

Images are treated as encounters, not decoration.

Ritual

Spiritual and cultural references become contemporary visual language.

Interface

Technology extends the artwork into interaction, documentation and experience.
