About the Artist

Visual Artist & Creative Technologist

Biography

Thomas Ogun is an interdisciplinary visual artist and creative technologist. His work explores identity, spirituality and memory through art, film, augmented reality and creative software.

Thomas Ogun Visuals brings these areas together in one studio practice: artworks, moving image, AR experiences, portraits and tools for creative people.

Mission

To make clear, meaningful work that helps people see identity, history and imagination in new ways.

Vision

To build a respected studio where art, technology and direction work together with purpose.

Creative Philosophy

Image, Memory and Interaction

Presence

Each image should feel direct, human and memorable.

Memory

Personal and cultural history shape the work.

Interaction

Technology adds movement, sound and new ways to experience the work.
